Odin Recipes

I’ve put together three recipes that reflect my love for different food cultures.

I started with a poké bowl, fresh and colorful, perfect for a healthy and balanced meal.
Then I made nasi goreng, simple but full of flavor, a classic comfort dish from Indonesia.
Finally, I created a recipe for Yemeni mandi, rich and aromatic, a dish that carries a sense of tradition and celebration.

By making these, I wanted to explore variety, from light and modern to deep and traditional, and show how cooking can connect different worlds on one table.
