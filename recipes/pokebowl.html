Poke Bowl

Poke Bowl- Fresh sushi rice topped with marinated raw
fish, crisp vegetables,
and classic garnishes for a light and
flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice (or short-grain rice)
  • Fresh raw fish (salmon or tuna, sushi-grade)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Rice vinegar
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Edamame
  • Seaweed (nori strips or wakame)
  • Green onion
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cook rice, season with rice vinegar.
  2. Dice fish, mix with soy sauce + sesame oil.
  3. Prep toppings: slice cucumber, avocado; cook edamame.
  4. Assemble bowl: rice at bottom, fish + toppings on top.
  5. Garnish with seaweed, green onion, sesame seeds.
