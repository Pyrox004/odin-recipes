Yemeni Mandhi

Here’s a recipe for Yemeni Mandi (مندي), a fragrant rice and meat dish traditionally cooked in a tandoor or underground pit,
but you can make it beautifully at home with your oven or stovetop.

Meat Marinade

  • Chicken or lamb (bone-in)
  • Oil or ghee
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Paprika or chili powder
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice

Rice

  • Basmati rice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oil or ghee
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Cardamom pods
  • Cloves
  • Bay leaves
  • Turmeric
  • Stock (chicken or lamb)
  • Salt

Garnish (optional but traditional)

  • Saffron + warm milk
  • Raisins
  • Slivered almonds

Steps

  1. Marinate meat with oil, lemon juice, salt, turmeric,
    cumin, coriander, paprika, cinnamon, cardamom, black pepper.
  2. Cook meat in oven (chicken ~40 min,
    lamb longer until tender) or grill.
  3. Sauté onion + garlic in oil/ghee,
    add cinnamon stick, cardamom, cloves, bay leaves.
  4. Add rice with turmeric + salt, stir.
  5. Pour stock, cover, cook until rice is fluffy.
  6. Place meat on rice, drizzle saffron milk (optional).
  7. Garnish with raisins + almonds.
    Serve hot.
